What this page does

This is a simple countdown tracking the thirty-day window following the publicly reported signing date of the legislation.

The goal is to provide a clear, at-a-glance timer that anyone can reference. No analysis, commentary, or claims are made about the contents of any records.

How the timer is calculated

The deadline is set to December 19, 2025 at 11:59:59 PM, thirty days after the reported signing date. Your local device time is used to calculate the remaining days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

If the official deadline is extended, changed, or records are released early, this timer may no longer reflect the live situation.
